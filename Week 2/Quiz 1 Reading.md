## Qualities of a tester
- Curious
- Experimental
- Fearless
- Intelligent
- Analytical 
- Learns fast
- Apolitical ie do not play politics
- Distrustful
- Question Everything

- Testers enjoy finding problems
- Testers want to be appreciated
- Testers can present problems and bugs well

- Testers ask a lot of questions

- Good project teams get testers in early so they can ask the right questions to save cost in the future.


## Role of a tester
- Some say role is to assure quality
- In essence the purpose is just to find bugs


## types of bugs?
- Cosmetic is like misspelled words, wrong color, etc
- not a fun kind of error lowkey
- People care about the cosmetic errors because of the irritation factor.
- Testers report every bug they find even the most mundane.
- But do not try to impact business priority.
- Tank errors as in complete ass errors that break shit
- Bugs are likely between intersections of developers code interacting with each other.

## How to take advantage of testers 
- Include them early to identify problems and ask the right questions
- listen to them
- Appreciate them
